Shisir Sapkota

Shisir Sapkota, widely known as Mama07, is a passionate engineering student and content creator from Nepal, blending technical precision with creative storytelling. His journey in civil engineering began early, stepping into the technical stream from class nine and graduating as a Sub-Engineer with hands-on construction field experience.Currently pursuing his engineering degree at Kathford International College of Engineering under IOE, Shisir continues to build his future with dedication and vision. Alongside academics, he launched his YouTube journey on September 15, 2021, where he shares engaging vlogs driven by passion and authenticity. With a strong foundation in engineering and a creative voice in digital content, his goal is to grow as a skilled engineer while inspiring others through his journey—proving that ambition, creativity, and consistency can shape a meaningful path forward. I share my engineering journey, struggles, lifestyle, and learning experiences.
